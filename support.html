Support

Thank you for using Care Planner.

Care Planner is a private care planner, pill tracker, medication reminder, symptom diary, health log, and daily care routine tracker.

If you need help, have questions, or want to report a problem, contact us at:

careplanner.support@gmail.com

When contacting support, please include:

  • your device model
  • your operating system version
  • app version
  • a short description of the issue
  • screenshots if helpful

Please do not send sensitive medical information unless it is necessary for your support request.

Important

Care Planner does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or dosage recommendations.

For medical questions, contact a qualified healthcare professional.

For emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately.
