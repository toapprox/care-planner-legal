Medical Disclaimer

Effective date: 2026

Care Planner is for personal health and care tracking only.

Care Planner may help you record and organize information such as medication schedules, pill reminders, dose logs, symptoms, blood pressure, glucose notes, migraine or pain notes, care tasks, and daily care routines.

Care Planner does not provide medical advice.

Care Planner does not provide:

  • diagnosis
  • treatment recommendations
  • dosage recommendations
  • medication safety advice
  • emergency medical assistance
  • professional medical judgment

Any information you enter into Care Planner is entered by you and is your responsibility.

You should always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making decisions about medications, symptoms, treatment, blood pressure, glucose, pain, migraine, health conditions, or care routines.

Do not ignore professional medical advice because of something recorded, displayed, or reminded by Care Planner.

Care Planner is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, monitor, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

Reminders and notifications may fail, arrive late, or be affected by device settings. Do not rely solely on Care Planner for critical medication or medical reminders.

If you think you or another person may be experiencing a medical emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.
